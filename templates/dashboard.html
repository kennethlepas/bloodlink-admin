Our Mission

BloodLink is dedicated to bridging the critical gap between blood donors and patients in need across Kenya and beyond. We believe every drop counts — and that technology can save lives by making blood donation more accessible, efficient, and community-driven.

This admin panel empowers your team to manage the platform, verify donors, monitor blood bank inventory, and ensure every request is fulfilled as fast as possible.

📖

Our Story

BloodLink was born out of a personal experience — watching a loved one struggle to find compatible blood during an emergency. Founded in 2024 in Nairobi, Kenya, our team set out to build a solution that works for every Kenyan, in any county, at any time.

What started as a small app idea has grown into a community of thousands of donors and requesters united by one goal: no one should die because blood wasn't available.
