Provider Operational Guidelines

As a registered healthcare facility on BloodLink, you are responsible for maintaining accurate inventory levels and ensuring all blood requests and referrals are medically verified. All administrative actions are logged for compliance auditing.

Hospital Admin Access

Your facility-level permissions and scope

🏥
Facility Administrator
Scoped to your designated health facility
✅ Authorized Operations:
  • Submit clinical blood requests
  • Initiate patient referrals to other banks
  • Update and sync local inventory
  • Direct chat with verified donors
  • View facility-specific analytics
  • Manage local notification settings

Restricted: No access to system-wide settings, other facility data, or platform user management.

Data Handling Policies

Compliance with health data regulations

1. Patient Confidentiality

Never share patient names or clinical documents outside of the BloodLink secure communication channels. Ensure all uploads only contain necessary medical information.

2. Donor Privacy

Donor contact information is provided solely for blood mobilization. Any secondary use of donor data is strictly prohibited and grounds for facility suspension.

Zero Tolerance: Misuse of donor registry or falsification of blood needs results in immediate system exclusion.
