Our Mission

BloodLink is committed to bridging the gap between blood donors and those in need across Kenya. We leverage technology to ensure reaching donors and managing blood requests is easier than ever, creating a seamless, verified, and efficient lifesaving network.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, BloodLink was born from a vision to ensure no Kenyan should die because blood wasn't available. This hospital portal empowers your facility to manage requests and inventory effectively.
